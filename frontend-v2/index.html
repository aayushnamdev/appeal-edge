Straight answers.

Do you guarantee reinstatement?

No one can, and anyone who promises it is lying to you. What we do is maximize your odds: identify the real root cause, write the plan of action Amazon actually wants to see, and guide it through.

How fast can my account come back?

It depends on the case type and how Amazon responds. Once we review your case, we tell you upfront what a realistic timeline looks like for your situation.

What does it cost?

Reviewing your case costs nothing. Pricing is discussed after we understand what your case actually requires, so you never pay for work you don't need.

Are you affiliated with Amazon?

No. Appeal Edge is an independent consultancy. We work for sellers, not for Amazon.

Who actually works on my case?
